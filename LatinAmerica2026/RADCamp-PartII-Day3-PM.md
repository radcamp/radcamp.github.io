# RADCamp NYC 2023 Part II (Bioinformatics)
# Day 3 (PM)

## Overview of the afternoon activities:
This is a free day for working on analyses for your data.
Refer to the [ipyrad analysis documentation](https://eaton-lab.org/ipyrad2/analyses/)
and the `ipyrad2 -h` command line help to see what other analysis
tools are available for you.

You should also use this time to be preparing your presentation for the
participant symposium tomorrow morning. Please create a new
google slides presentation in the [Participant Symposium Presentations](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1TeDLweZlBW7VOdF-oGX_igBiP46GlvE0?usp=drive_link) google drive folder. Try to aim for 5-7
minutes including background on the system, hypotheses, and
results.
